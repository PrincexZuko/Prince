About me

Who is Princeton Gill? Thats a very good question! who am i? I'm a 20-year-old adult from the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) in Canada. I was born and raised in the GTA by my two immigrant parents, my mom who is from Ghana and my pops who is from Trinidad and Tobago. Growing up, I was exposed to a rich blend of cultures and traditions from both sides of my family. I spent most of my life living in the GTA, but recently decided to move to London, Ontario with my parents to attend college. It was a big change for me, but I embraced the opportunity to explore a new city and pursue his passion for computer programming. In my spare time,I enjoys exploring new places, meeting new people, and trying new things. I loves to travel and hopes to one day visit every continent in the world. I also enjoys playing video games, watching movies, and listening to music. Overall, I'm a young man on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. I am very excited to see where life takes me and looking forward to sharing my adventures with others.

College Life

Im current in rolled at Fanshawe college and It offers a variety of programs in different fields, including business, health sciences, technology, and arts. The program im currently taking is CPA3 a course in the Computer Programming Analysis program offered by Fanshawe College. This course is designed to teach students the fundamentals of computer programming, including data types, control structures, functions, and arrays. It also covers object-oriented programming concepts, such as classes, inheritance, and polymorphism. By taking this course, you will gain the knowledge and skills needed to develop computer programs using popular programming languages like Java, C++, and Python. You will also learn how to use programming tools like Integrated Development Environments (IDEs) and version control systems. This course is ideal for those who are interested in pursuing a career in computer programming or software development. Overall, Fanshawe College is a great place to learn and grow in your chosen field, and taking CPA3 is an excellent step towards achieving your career goals in computer programming analysis.

Travel Adventures

One of my biggest passions is traveling and exploring new places, especially in Canada. I love to discover the unique cultures and traditions of different regions within my own country. Recently, I had the opportunity to visit Quebec and Montreal, two vibrant and historic cities in Eastern Canada.

In Quebec, I was fascinated by the French language and culture, which is a significant part of the province's heritage. The cobblestone streets, charming cafes, and picturesque buildings of Old Quebec City transported me back in time. The Montmorency Falls, a natural wonder located just outside the city, was a breathtaking sight to behold.

Montreal, on the other hand, was a bustling metropolis with a mix of modern architecture and historic landmarks. I visited the Notre-Dame Basilica, a stunning example of Gothic Revival architecture, and enjoyed some of the city's famous poutine, a delicious dish of french fries, cheese curds, and gravy.

Exploring Quebec and Montreal gave me a better appreciation for the diverse beauty of Canada and its people. I am grateful for the opportunity to have traveled to these cities and look forward to discovering more of what this great country has to offer.
